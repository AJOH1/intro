About Me

My name is Ekeh Michael, and I hail from the vibrant city of Enugu, Nigeria.
My passion for coding ignited during my junior years of education, igniting a spark that has continued to burn brightly ever since.
The allure of crafting digital solutions, the thrill of problem-solving through code, and the limitless potential of technology captured my imagination.
I immersed myself in the world of programming, dedicating countless hours to learning and experimenting.
As I journey forward, I aspire to become a skilled and respected software developer. I dream of venturing overseas, where I can collaborate with talented individuals from around the world and contribute to groundbreaking projects.
With each line of code I write, I am one step closer to realizing my ambitions.
